What Daso is

Daso is a purpose-built computer for kids ages 7 to 10. It is designed to help kids read, write, draw, code, and stay connected with the people they love, without turning every spare minute into screen time. It is in development, not shipping yet.

The idea starts with a simple concern: adult technology was not built around childhood. Banning every device is understandable, but it leaves families negotiating around tools shaped for grown-ups, advertising, and attention.

Parents should be in the room while this is still a question. We need the hopeful, the skeptical, and the tired of negotiating screen rules to say what feels right, what feels wrong, and what would actually help at home.

About Max

He grew up with six brothers in a family where technology helped his mom raise seven boys. Daso comes from that same practical place: a calmer, more intentional computer for childhood.
